The Simple Explanation

FICA Tip Credit in Plain English

When your employees report tips to you, you're required to pay the employer portion of FICA taxes (7.65%) on those tips. The FICA Tip Credit lets you get a portion of that money backâ€”specifically for tips that push an employee's hourly rate above $7.25/hour (the federal minimum wage).

Why Does This Credit Exist?

Congress created this credit in 1993 to help restaurant and hospitality businesses. Here's the problem they were trying to solve:

  • You pay employees a low cash wage (often $2.13-$5/hour depending on your state)
  • Customers tip your employees to make up the difference
  • Your employees report those tips to you (as required by law)
  • Now you have to pay FICA taxes (Social Security and Medicare) on ALL those tipsâ€”even though you didn't actually pay your employees that money

Congress recognized this was a burden on restaurant owners, so they created a tax credit to offset some of those taxes. It's not a loopholeâ€”it's an intentional benefit designed specifically for your industry.

How Much Can You Get Back?

The credit equals 7.65% of tips that exceed what's needed to bring an employee's total pay up to $7.25/hour.

Who Qualifies?

You likely qualify if you operate:

  • Restaurants (full-service, casual dining, fine dining)
  • Bars, taverns, nightclubs
  • Coffee shops and cafes
  • Hotels (room service, restaurants, bellhops)
  • Catering companies
  • Any food/beverage business where tipping is customary

Business size doesn't matter. Even if you have just 3-5 employees receiving tips, you can qualify for thousands in credits.

How to Claim It

To claim the FICA Tip Credit, you need to:

  1. Gather documentation: Payroll records, tip reports, Forms 941
  2. Complete Form 8846: This is the IRS form for calculating the credit
  3. File with your tax return: For current year, or file amended returns for past years
  4. Receive your refund: Either via IRS processing (3-6 months) or expedited funding (2-4 weeks)

Most restaurant owners work with specialists (like us) who handle the entire process, so you can focus on running your business.
